# 📌 What signing means

By signing, you affirm that you:

- Support human accountability in AI-assisted development.
- Endorse explicit design and deep validation.
- Recognize the importance of preserving engineering skill formation.
- Agree that automation must amplify — not replace — engineering judgment.

Signing is a professional and ethical commitment. It does not create legal obligations.
